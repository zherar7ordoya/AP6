2.0  VISSIM/ANALYZE DOCUMENTATION UPDATES
*****************************************************
How to analyze a system with a timeDelay block: Replace
the timeDelay block with a Pade approximation. There
are four Pade approximations (first through fourth order).
They are located in the VISSIM30\TOOLBOX\PADE subdirectory.
